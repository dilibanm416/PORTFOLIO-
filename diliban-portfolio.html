Scene 01

MY STORY

Stories have the power to change how we see the world — and it is my responsibility to tell them with honesty, dignity, and love.

My Passion for Stories

I'm Diliban from Chennai. For me, visual storytelling isn't just a job — it's a responsibility. I am deeply passionate about sharing tales that focus on representation, inclusivity, and the lives of people who are often overlooked.

The Heart of Filmmaking

I believe the true purpose of filmmaking is to capture honest stories about where we come from and the communities we belong to. My goal is to create films that inspire others and treat every person's story with the integrity and dignity it deserves.

My Vision

I have many stories to tell, all with the same goal: to make sure everyone feels seen and heard through the power of film. From the streets of Chennai to the villages of Tamil Nadu — every life holds a universe worth capturing.
